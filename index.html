gardening tips

gardening is a very common hobby but sometimes it goes wron

if you're starting a new garden use this step by step instruction

to make you're dream garden true.

step 1.cut holes 1 inch wider than your plant,dont make it to wide or you won't have enough fertilizer

step 2.put in your plant in the middle of the hole ,don't put stress on the roots when puttting it in

step 3.put in fertilizer around the plants and minerals

step 4.water your plants depending on what they are tropical plants need to be watered every day,cactuses need to be watered

every week,temperate plants need to be watered every 2 days, trees need to be watered every day

tips:

put the largest plants at the back and the smallest at the front

put your plants in the sun

if you have a big budget you can install solar powered lights for the night
